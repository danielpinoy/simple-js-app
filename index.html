A Pokédex is a handheld electronic encyclopedia specifically designed to catalog, record, and provide essential details about Pokémon species. It is an essential companion for Pokémon Trainers on their journey to become Pokémon Masters. The device resembles a compact computer or smartphone, featuring a screen, buttons, and a user-friendly interface.
